Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Overview

Gigbag ("the app") is a musician toolkit developed by Pepsin Selvaraj. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Gigbag does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

Microphone Access

Gigbag requests microphone access solely to detect pitch for the chromatic and guitar tuner features. Audio is processed entirely on your device in real time. No audio data is recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

Analytics & Tracking

Gigbag does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking libraries. No usage data is collected.

Third-Party Services

Gigbag does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Gigbag does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at pepsins@icloud.com.
